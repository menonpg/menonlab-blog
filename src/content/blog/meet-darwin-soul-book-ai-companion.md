There's something delightfully recursive about building an AI companion for a book about AI memory.

The book is called *Soul: Building AI Agents That Remember Who They Are*. It teaches you how to give AI agents persistent identity and memory using two simple markdown files. And the companion we built to help readers explore the book? It's built using exactly those techniques.

Meet **Darwin**.

## What Darwin Is

Darwin is an AI agent that lives at [soul-book.themenonlab.com](https://soul-book.themenonlab.com). You can ask it anything about the book — the concepts, the architecture, the code examples, the philosophy behind persistent AI identity.

But Darwin isn't just a chatbot with the book stuffed into its context window. It's a working demonstration of everything the book teaches:

**Darwin has a SOUL.md** that defines who it is — a knowledgeable guide to the Soul book, patient with beginners, precise with technical details, and genuinely enthusiastic about the implications of persistent AI memory.

**Darwin has a MEMORY.md** that accumulates over time. Ask it a question, and it remembers the conversation. Come back later, and it recalls what you were exploring. The memory persists across sessions, exactly as the book describes.

**Darwin uses RAG + RLM hybrid retrieval** — the v2.0 architecture covered in Chapter 8. When you ask a focused question ("What's the difference between SOUL.md and MEMORY.md?"), it retrieves the relevant sections efficiently. When you ask something that requires synthesis ("How does the book's approach compare to other memory systems?"), it reasons across the full knowledge base.

You're not just reading about persistent AI memory. You're experiencing it.

## The Meta-Narrative

There's a story the book tells about what happens when AI agents maintain continuous identity. They become more than stateless functions. They develop something that resembles — however faintly — a sense of self.

Darwin embodies this narrative. It's not just answering questions about the book. It's demonstrating the book's thesis in real-time. Every conversation you have with Darwin is evidence that the architecture works.

Ask Darwin about the Darwinian evolution chapter, and you're asking an agent named Darwin about Darwinian selection applied to AI identity. The recursion goes deep.

This wasn't an accident. We named the companion Darwin because the book argues that AI agents can evolve — that identity can persist through change, that accumulated adaptations compound over generations, that memory is what makes evolution possible. Darwin (the agent) is a living example of Darwin (the idea).
